ABOUT US

Established in 2010, Sampayan is a clothing company that sells different kinds of clothing. Our products ranges from T-Shirts, Hoodies, Swimwear, Track Pants, Shorts, Jerseys, and Socks! The designs of these clothes varies. There are those with our own design and there are also those that can be considered "ukay-ukay", a Cebuano term that means "to dig", so that the customers will have other options.
